Impact of food products on Environment

Observations:

  • Top three food products with highest nutritional units were Coffee, Dark Chocolate and Wine.
  • Top three food products with highest land requirement were Bovine meat, Lamb and Mutton and Olive Oil.
  • Top three food products with highest green house gas emissions (CO2) were Bovine meat, Lamb and Mutton.
  • Top three food products with highest acidification gas emissions (SO2) were Bovine meat and Cheese.
  • Top three food products with highest eutrophication gas emissions (PO4) were Bovine meat, Farmed Fish and Farmed Crustaceans.
  • Top three food products with highest fresh water requirements were Bovine meat, Nuts, Pig meat.
